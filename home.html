Library

The Corelia Library has been collecting the works of contemporary composers for over 40 years. The collection, the most comprehensive of its kind, is the only source for many of these recordings and scores and can be viewed by siging or loging with us.

Benfits

The key benefits of representation are the inclusion of artists' works and associated materials in the Corelia's collection which, in the new online environment, encourages a focus not only on the work and the artist biography, but on the broader documentation of the context for the work.

About Us

The Corelia Project houses and curates the most comprehensive catalogue of music around the world, a living collection of composings, recordings, research papers, teaching kits, classroom materials and aggregated media. The collection includes more than 30,000 items by approximately 1000 artists from around the world.
